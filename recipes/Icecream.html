Flavorful Icecream

a sweet, frozen dessert made from dairy (milk/cream), sugar, and flavorings, churned to incorporate air for a smooth, creamy texture, resulting in a cold, scoopable treat popular worldwide as a snack or dessert, with varieties like gelato or non-dairy options

Ingredients

  • 600ml double cream
  • Â½ a 397g can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps to prepare the dish

  1. Combine the condensed milk, cream, and vanilla in a large bowl
  2. Beat with an electric whisk until the mixture is thick and stiff, resembling clotted cream.
  3. Scrape the mixture into a freezer-safe container or a large loaf tin.
  4. Cover with cling film (plastic wrap) and a lid.
  5. Freeze until solid (around 6 hours or overnight).
