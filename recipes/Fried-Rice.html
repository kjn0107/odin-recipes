Fried Rice

Delicious Zimbabwean Fried Rice

A versatile Asian dish of cooked rice. creating a flavorful, often smoky, meal or side dish, ideally made with leftover, day-old rice for best texture.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Peas
  • Carrots
  • Onions
  • Pork

Steps to prepare the dish

  1. Break up any large clumps of cold, day-old rice with your fingers.
  2. Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a wok or large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Add the beaten eggs, scramble until fully cooked, and remove them to a separate plate.
  3. Add 1-2 tablespoons of oil to the hot pan. Add the onions, peas, and carrots (and minced garlic/ginger if using). Stir-fry until the vegetables are tender, about 3 minutes.
  4. Increase the heat to high. Add the remaining oil to the pan, then add the cold rice. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes, breaking up any remaining clumps, until the rice is heated through and begins to brown in places.
  5. Pour in the soy sauce, sesame oil, and any additional protein (if using). Add the cooked eggs back into the pan. Toss everything together for 1-2 minutes until everything is well combined and evenly coated.
  6. Remove from heat, stir in the green onions, and serve immediately.
